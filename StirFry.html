Vege stir-fry Recipe

Recipe

Ingredients: Rice, carrot, bamboo shoots, peas, brcolli, corn.

  1. Boil rice

    Fill pot with water until it jst covers the rice and boil over high heat.

  2. Stir-Fry veges

    Place all the veges in a pan with a splash of oil over a low-medium heat and continuously stir around to avoid burning.

  3. Check rice

    Every so often check the rice and add water until the rice is "el dente".

  4. Mix all together

    Once the rice is ready, add it to the vege pan and mix it all together.
