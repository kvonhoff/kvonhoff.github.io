Garlic Bread Recipe

Recipe

Ingredients: french stick, garlic butter.

  1. Cut up the french stick

    Using a bread knife, cut up the french stick along the small side and stopping before readching the bottom.

  2. Butter french stick

    Butter the french stick with garlic butter.

  3. Wrap in aluminum foil

    Wrap the entire stick in aluminum foil shiny side facing in.

  4. Bake

    Bake in the oven at 190c for 15 minutes.
