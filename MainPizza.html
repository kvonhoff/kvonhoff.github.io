Pizza Recipe

Recipe

Ingredients: Pizza base, tomato paste, grated mozzarella cheese, feta cheese, sliced musrooms, spinach, sliced sundried ttomato, chopped capcicum, egg, chopped onoin, BBQ sauce, shaved parmesan cheese.

  1. Paste pizza

    Evenly spread tomato paste over pizza, add BBQ sauce if desired.

  2. Place toppings

    Place all non-cheese toppings on pizza.

  3. Sprinkle cheese

    Sprinkle cheese over the top and add feta if desired.

  4. bake

    Bake at 190c for 20 minutes.
